Upload Invoices from Google Drive

Paste the URL of a Google Drive folder containing PDF invoices. Make sure the folder is shared with your Google account.
How to get the folder URL:
  1. Open Google Drive and navigate to your folder
  2. Right-click the folder and select "Share"
  3. Ensure you have access to the folder
  4. Copy the folder URL from your browser's address bar
